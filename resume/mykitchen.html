The Love I Have For Food And Travel


FOOD

I started being interested in food when I was around 15 years old. It was when I saw my mother doing everything that we need at home. One day she was out to see a relative. she was exhausted the moment she got home. I decided to cook something she loves to eat. It was my first time to do stand by the stove. I took 2 hours to cook, which needs only 15 minutes. as soon as my mother tasted it, she told me that it was so delicious. I was excited to see her happy, not the food. Since then I love cooking at home, rather than eating in a restaurant.


TRAVEL

When I was a teacher I have been to different places, for teaching as well as to visit friends. Every travel was interesting. All I met were different teachers, different people, places and culture. It inspired me that there is a lot more places to visit around the globe. I always plan on travelling to places of my favorite.
